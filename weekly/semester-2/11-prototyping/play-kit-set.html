Introduction

01 Feb 2023
Due to issues with the blender paper model export for my 3D models, I downloaded two paper templates online.

In order to make the bird and tree models, I used paper model templates I found online. The blender feature for exporting paper models did not work out due to the complexity of the exported template.

Since there were so many parts to the template, it took me the entire day to cut and fold it. I used the wrong paper thickness, so folding it was difficult because the paper dents easily.

Conductive Paint

The models were painted with conductive paint. Some of the painted surfaces were not very responsive due to too much dilation of the paint.

Upon connecting the cables to the touch board, my paper models were not able to stand properly due to the added weight of the cables. I had to use tape to secure the models.
